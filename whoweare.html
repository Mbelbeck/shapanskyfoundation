Who we are

Kerry Shapansky is a Toronto based philanthropist and investor. A serial entrepreneur, Kerry previously was founder and CEO for one of Canada’s largest marketing companies and successfully sold it to a private equity firm.

Mandy Shapansky is a Toronto based philanthropist and corporate director. Previously Mandy spent 30 years with a local technology company culminating in five years a President and CEO of their Canadian subsidiary. Mandy was previously named one of Canada’s 100 Most Powerful Women.

Founded in 2011, Shapansky Foundation is a private charitable foundation. In 2017 Kerry and Mandy Shapansky’s leadership and contributions to the Small and Growing Business (SGB) platform were recognized with The Power of Generosity Award from World Vision Canada.
